The Beginning

We find ourselves on an Autumn day in Alfbern, a city we already know!

  • Arrived last night/afternoon at the Headless Vargouille Inn.
  • Monastery of Trea'mor in Alfbern, with 5 monks living there.
  • Amit is the half-elven innkeeper, having a fight outside with a dwarven man (something about barrels?), Roy Earthdweller, the local wheel-maker.
  • Roy says Amit has always been respectable, but Roy is getting married, his family is throwing a party and they need "the good stuff". But 2 barrels of #Palinka (strong spirit) went missing.
  • There were guards watching the carts the barrels were loaded onto but they claim not to have seen anything
  • Finvelinor and Melinor both may have heard the sounds of rumbling (rolling of barrels), possibly another cart moving around, the preceding evening.
  • Courtyard is rectangular with cobblestones. Frequently used, not often repaired.
    • 2 carts closer to Roy's house
    • 1 human guard from fire brigade. She sits on a stone near the wagons.
  • She claims to still be waking up (whoops!) and complains about the bright light. No sign of hangover, but she seems severely exhausted, more than what one should be after a night of drinking or guarding or whatever.
  • Says the chief of the fire brigade is super busy, but everything got very busy 5 days ago? But the chief hasn't explained why. He started going on longer expeditions, often outside of the city taking care of who knows what. Thought the chief was dealing with the situation with Merike as usual, but ze has to sleep a lot (because ancient), and whenever we use zir help it has to be managed as a limited resource. Ze sleeps in town but also in a cave a day's walk away in the mountains.
  • Finvelinor checks Beverly for signs of being drugged. Romero looks around for signs of magic, like sleeping spells, and Melinor investigates the area for tracks.
  • Romero senses a kind of divine energy present, vibes of if you cooked soup several hours ago and could still smell it in the area.
  • Finvelinor tries divine sense and "smells" it's strongest where the barrels were and can follow the traces (wispy cartoon smell) to a gap in the stone fence of the courtyard, to a field and forest by the time the ability ends.
  • Continuing forward we see light and smell smoke/fire in what is probably a clearing.

Combat

  • Romero runs to put out the fire with his blanket
  • Finvelinor senses a divine force pushing the barrels and calls out to the others
  • Melinor runs to tackle a barrel but misses
  • One of the barrels spits acid? at Fin, but misses, the other sprays flaming alcohol which Fin takes right to the face.
  • Romero rolls over some fire to put it out
  • Finvelinor puts out his own fire then lasso's one of the barrels and tries to speak to it gently to calm it down. Hears gurgling in response.
  • Melinor tries to lasso his own but misses.
  • Romero speaks down to them like children to make them stop playing but it has no effect.
  • Barrel breaks free from Fin
  • Finvelinor commands one of the barrels to freeze and runs and grabs the rope still attached to the other.
  • Melinor manages to remove the top of one barrel and finds a glowing shrimp creature swimming around inside.
  • Finvelinor's barrel spits a line of burning alcohol, Melinor saves but Fin takes another face full of fire and falls prone again with only 3 HP remaining.
  • Romero tries to get the shrimp out of the barrel but misses. It laughs at him.
  • Finvelinor gets the flames off himself and moves to the other side of the barrel, then heals himself by laying on hands.
  • Melinor manages to fish the shrimp out of his barrel, which exclaims "Fuck" in celestial (only Romero understands).
  • The other barrel sprays fire and this barrel catches fire. But Romero tries to put it out. Fails, but threatens the shrimp with putting it in his alms box. The shrimp claims to just be doing what it's told, that we don't know how to deal with fire and that it's teaching us?
  • Finvelinor tries to command and intimidate the second shrimp but to no avail.
  • A dark-skinned dwarven man arrives with a barrel backpack complaining about the fire. Fires his hose and sprays the barrel and Melinor. This knocks over the barrel which isn't perfectly sealed, and it begins leaking.
  • Melinor rights the barrel and runs the shrimp away.
  • Romero heals Mel with a word, jumps over some fire and tries to pry the lid of the other barrel off with a dagger but doesn't quite manage (lowers the DC)
  • Finvelinor gets the lid off and snatches the shrimp out, then runs over to the rocks.
  • The dwarf sprays the fire but gets knocked down by the force and doesn't get it all.
  • Melinor tries to cut off the shrimp's head with his dagger but can't get through the carapace.
  • The shrimps try to spit acid on us but both fail.
  • Romero rolls the fire out.
  • Finvelinor slices partway through but doesn't manage to kill it.
  • Dwarf grumbles about newbies who care more about wine than protecting the community and we tell him off.
  • Melinor finally manages to sneak attack, then throws a knife and hits Fin's.

End combat

  • Romero explains to us that one of the shrimps mentioned they were "training" us to handle fire, on the orders of their "boss".
  • This dwarf is the chief of the fire brigade😱.
  • Says Divination wizards claim the volcano is extinct and won't erupt again, but Perforus is telling his followers to get ready for when it erupts again. God is trying to get the whole region ready, not just his followers, by sending fire elementals and little tricky shrimp like this. (Says Perforus is a nice guy, in the fairy tales and myths, but kinda hard to deal with like this 😅)
  • Gives us one vial of ointment each for treating burns - 1d12+3 healing from fire damage.
  • IS THE SUN A GIANT FLAMING EGG WITH A CREATURE GROWING INSIDE?

Wedding

  • Arrive at wedding before ceremony. Melinor sneaks almost unnaturally well into the wedding.

  • Officiated by a firbolg woman (Sister Graelea) with strong build and long flowy blue dress. Religion is basically 'vibes' 😅

  • Adorning bride (Anya) with blue flowers (like blue bells and tiny not-violets). Becomes flower shoulder-pads.

  • Roy carried out on his brothers' arms making a chair.

  • Anya kneeling on one knee.

  • Roy leaps from the seat, runs and leaps into Anya's arms who holds him up in the air like a dance lift. Anya not a super strong woman but obviously been practicing.

  • Lowers him down, they embrace.

  • Sister Graelea takes one of the flowers from the shoulder pads and unwinds them, re-winding them around the couple.

  • Then the couple teleports away and Sister Graelea gives a speech about them making their "first connection" as a married couple, that it's a conversation. (We all know she means sex 😉)

  • Party begins. Dinner without the couple. Then dancing, with a dance in a circle with the close family, then at the height the couple reappears in different outfits.

  • Roy comes and talks to us while Anya is dancing with her sisters and thanks us for getting the wine.

  • Finvelinor chats up Sister Graelea, they dance for a while and she "invites him back to her shrine" 🍆💦

  • Melinor distances himself from the festivities a bit and

  • Romero sits by himself contemplatively, looking a bit unhappy. Brother Leywin (high elf) joins him. Romero opens up about his feelings, not knowing what he still doesn't have or what it could have felt like, what he lost. Brother Leywin thinks it's already beautiful that Trea'mor brought him back.

  • Romero's mentor left for volcano, trying to work out holy water logistics due to vampire problem.

  • Finvelinor and Romero find Melinor sitting staring at an old knife he never uses for anything. Has told Fin it was a gift from someone important and it's meant to keep him safe. Wooden hilt with an M carved in one side and an E on the other side.

  • Saboh comes in at the moment Romero is trying to light straw on fire and tells him off. But Romero convinces him to let them take a key to the fire brigade practice room to mess around with fire. Needless to say they're quite drunk at this point...

  • Mel tries to drunkenly add a flower to Anya's hair but she catches him. When he explains how pretty she looks and he just wanted to add a flower, she asks him to put it in her hair. Anya expresses her gratitude for bringing back the Palinka — her sisters were unsure about Roy.

  • Mel and Rom experiment the rest of the night lighting different qualities of Palinka on fire...
